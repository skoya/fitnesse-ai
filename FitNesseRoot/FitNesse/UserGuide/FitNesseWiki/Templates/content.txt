Templates make it easy to reuse common page scaffolding. Create template pages under any !-TemplateLibrary-! page, then insert them while editing.

!3 Creating templates
Create a page named !-TemplateLibrary-! and add child pages under it. Each child is a template.

Example:
{{{.Project.TemplateLibrary.ApiTest}}}

!3 Inserting templates while editing
Open a page and click '''Edit'''. Use the Templates panel:
* Select a template from the list.
* Click '''Insert''' to paste it at the caret (or replace the selection).
* Click '''Edit''' to update the template itself.

!3 Example template page
This page can live under a !-TemplateLibrary-!:
{{{!define TEST_SYSTEM {slim}

!|script|ExampleFixture|
|start|${ENV}|
|step|value|
}}}
