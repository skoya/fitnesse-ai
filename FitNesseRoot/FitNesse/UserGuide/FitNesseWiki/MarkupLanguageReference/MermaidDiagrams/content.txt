Mermaid diagrams can be embedded directly in a page using a dedicated block.

'''Markup Text'''
{{{!mermaid
graph TD
  A[Start] --> B{Decision}
  B -->|Yes| C[Do thing]
  B -->|No| D[Skip]
!endmermaid}}}

'''Displays as:'''
!mermaid
graph TD
  A[Start] --> B{Decision}
  B -->|Yes| C[Do thing]
  B -->|No| D[Skip]
!endmermaid

!note Mermaid rendering runs in the browser. The diagram text is rendered after the page loads.
